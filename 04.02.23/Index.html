Funtion



A funtion is like when you have a to-do list full of tasks, such as do the dishes
1- Organize the dishes
2- take the sponge and put soup on top
3- turn on the water
4- wet the sponge 
5- scrub the sponge on the first item
6- drain the dirty with the water
7- put it to dry 
To resume all of that we just write do the dishes
The next task is walk with the dog, and we alredy know how to do this because the steps are storage in our system
So in our daily to-do list we only write the name to call the task, imagine have to write every day all stpes of a task, would be huge
A funtion works like that, it breaks down a complex task into smaller steps, to be more organized and readable, and easier to call in other time.
